Shipping Policy

How rates, handling, tracking, delays, and international delivery work.

TermsPrivacyShippingReturns

Address-based rates

EasyPost returns available carrier rates using the destination address and the inventory item's packed weight, length, width, and height. The selected transportation charge is shown before Stripe payment. Carrier adjustments may apply when an address is corrected or the actual parcel differs from its configured measurements.

Address responsibility

Review the exact name and address before payment. Contact support@crackpacks.com immediately if a correction is needed. We cannot promise changes after a label is purchased. Reshipment caused by an incorrect customer-provided address, refusal, or unclaimed package may require an additional charge.

Processing and delivery estimates

In-stock orders normally leave within 2–5 business days. Preorders and items marked with a future release date ship after stock is received. Carrier delivery days are estimates unless the chosen service expressly guarantees them. If we cannot ship within the time stated at purchase, we will provide an updated date and the cancellation or refund options required by applicable law.

Tracking and manual labels

Crack Packs purchases labels manually. When tracking is attached, the member receives an email from orders@crackpacks.com and can view private tracking under Profile → Orders. A carrier may take up to 48 hours to show its first scan.

Loss, damage, and delivery scans

Inspect deliveries promptly. Report visible damage, missing contents, or a package marked delivered but not received to support@crackpacks.com within 7 days, with the order number, packaging photographs, and any carrier information. Keep all packaging while a carrier claim is reviewed.

International delivery

International product prices exclude shipping and import charges. Unless expressly stated otherwise, orders ship DAP (formerly DDU): the recipient is the importer of record and pays destination duties, taxes, brokerage, customs clearance, and carrier collection charges. Currency conversions displayed on the site are estimates; Stripe, the card issuer, or the customer's bank may use a different rate.

Restricted destinations

We may decline destinations or services that carriers, payment providers, sanctions rules, customs requirements, fraud controls, or product restrictions do not support. Any resulting unshipped order will be refunded to the original payment method.
