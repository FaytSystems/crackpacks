Privacy Policy

How Crack Packs collects, uses, secures, and shares account and commerce data.

TermsPrivacyShippingReturns

Information collected

We collect account email, name, birthday, Crack Packs User ID, verification status, referral and reward activity, device/passkey public credential data, order history, shipping contact and address, selected carrier service, tracking events, support messages, and basic security logs such as a protected IP hash. Stripe receives payment and identity-verification details; Crack Packs does not store full card numbers or full identity-document images.

How information is used

We use data to authenticate accounts, prevent duplicate or abusive accounts, process payments, reserve inventory, calculate shipping, fulfill and track orders, issue refunds, operate referrals and rewards, provide support, send transactional notices, secure the service, maintain records, and comply with legal obligations. Promotional messages should be sent only where permitted and include opt-out instructions.

Service providers and disclosures

Data is shared as needed with Cloudflare for hosting, security, email routing, and database services; Stripe for checkout, fraud controls, taxes when enabled, and refunds; EasyPost and participating carriers for rates and tracking; Resend or Cloudflare Email for messages; and professional or government recipients when legally required. We do not sell personal information for money.

Retention and security

We retain order, payment-reference, refund, and tax records as reasonably needed for accounting, disputes, fraud prevention, and law. Shipping and support information is retained only as needed for those purposes. Security includes signed provider webhooks, restricted owner tools, verified accounts, passkeys, rate limits, encrypted transport, secret storage, and access logging. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and local storage

The site uses browser storage for signed-in sessions, security state, referral or offer attribution, and preferences. Cloudflare and Stripe may use cookies or similar technology for fraud prevention, checkout, and service operation. Blocking required storage can prevent login or payment.

Choices and rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or an export of appropriate account data by contacting support@crackpacks.com. Some records must be retained for completed transactions, security, taxes, disputes, or other legal reasons. Marketing opt-outs do not stop necessary order, security, or account messages.

Children and international users

The service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. International data may be processed in the United States and other locations used by our providers. Local mandatory privacy rights remain applicable.

Contact and changes

Questions or privacy requests: support@crackpacks.com. We may update this policy prospectively and will change the effective date when material revisions are posted.
