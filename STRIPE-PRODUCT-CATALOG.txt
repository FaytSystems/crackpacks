CRACK PACKS - STRIPE PRODUCT AND INVENTORY SETUP
Updated: July 18, 2026

IMPORTANT
Stripe Checkout is generated from the protected Crack Packs inventory database.
You do NOT have to create duplicate Stripe Products manually for checkout to work.
The website sends the inventory name, current protected website price, quantity,
and selected EasyPost shipping service to Stripe from the server.

If you also want products visible in the Stripe Dashboard Product Catalog, use:

  CRACK PACKS | [SET] | [PRODUCT TYPE] | [PACK COUNT OR VARIANT]

Examples:
  CRACK PACKS | Mega Evolution - Pitch Black | Booster Box | 36 Packs
  CRACK PACKS | Mega Evolution - Pitch Black | Elite Trainer Box
  CRACK PACKS | Mega Evolution - Pitch Black | Booster Bundle | 6 Packs

Use a separate Stripe Product for each physical package size. Do not reuse one
Stripe price for a booster pack, bundle, ETB, and booster box because EasyPost
requires the packed weight and dimensions of the exact item being ordered.

Recommended Stripe metadata:
  crackpacks_inventory_slug = copy the Public Slug from Master Dashboard
  upc = UPC digits, if known
  category = Booster Box / ETB / Bundle / Pack / Tin / Case / Single
  fulfillment = manual_label
  shipping_rate_source = easypost

Do not enter packed dimensions in Stripe. Enter them in:
  MASTER DASHBOARD > INVENTORY > Edit Product > Packed shipment details

WEIGHING RULE
Pack the product exactly as it will be mailed, including the shipping box,
bubble wrap, padding, inserts, bonuses, and label. Then record:
  Weight = total packed ounces
  Length = longest outside box edge in inches
  Width  = second-longest outside box edge in inches
  Height = shortest outside box edge in inches

EasyPost uses those four values plus the customer's address to calculate the
shipping services and prices displayed before Stripe Checkout.
