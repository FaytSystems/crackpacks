Returns & Refunds

Clear rules for cancellations, sealed collectibles, errors, and damage.

TermsPrivacyShippingReturns

Cancellation before shipment

Request cancellation promptly at support@crackpacks.com. If the order is still processing and no label/tracking has been attached, the owner can issue a full Stripe refund. Once shipped, the return rules below apply.

Eligible returns

Unopened, factory-sealed, regularly stocked products may be requested for return within 14 calendar days after delivery. Items must be unused and in the original undamaged retail packaging. Contact support before sending anything; unauthorized returns may be refused.

Normally non-returnable items

Opened packs, boxes, tins, cases, or other randomized products; live-ripped products; individual cards or slabs whose protective packaging has been opened or altered; personalized items; promotional gifts; redeemed rewards; and clearance or final-sale items are normally non-returnable unless damaged, incorrect, counterfeit, or otherwise covered by mandatory law.

Wrong, damaged, or materially misdescribed orders

Contact support within 7 days after delivery with the order number and clear photographs of the product, shipping box, cushioning, and label. Do not discard packaging. When verified, we will provide an appropriate replacement, return arrangement, partial refund, or full refund without removing rights provided by law.

Return shipping and deductions

For a change-of-mind approved return, the customer pays tracked return shipping and original shipping is not refundable unless required by law. For our error or qualifying transit damage, Crack Packs will provide or reimburse reasonable return shipping. Refunds may be reduced only for documented loss in value caused by handling beyond what was necessary to inspect an eligible product.

Refund timing

Approved refunds go to the original Stripe payment method. We normally submit them within 3–5 business days after approval or return inspection. Banks and card networks control when the credit appears. Shipping a return does not itself guarantee approval.

Chargebacks

Please contact support first so we can investigate. Nothing here prevents a lawful card dispute, but knowingly filing a false dispute or retaining both merchandise and refunded funds may result in account restriction and evidence being provided to the payment processor.
