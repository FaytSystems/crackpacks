First-time seller setup
OBS setup

Create your Crack Packs key once, paste it into OBS, and you are ready to reuse the same connection for future shows until you intentionally rotate it.

  1. Click Create key to generate your private Crack Packs stream input.
  2. Open OBS and go to Settings > Stream.
  3. Set Service to Custom.
  4. Paste the RTMPS server into OBS Server.
  5. Paste the Stream key into OBS Stream Key.
  6. Save OBS. Future shows reuse this same saved key until you choose Regenerate stream key.
  7. Your key is tied to your seller account and the live input stays locked outside your active show window.
  8. Start streaming in OBS before opening auctions on the site.

After your first successful key creation, this guide stays tucked away unless you open it with the OBS setup guide button.

If Cloudflare creation fails, the guide still applies once the stream input is fixed. The most common causes are a missing Stream token permission or the Stream service not being enabled on the Cloudflare account.
